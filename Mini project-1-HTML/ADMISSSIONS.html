Admissions

In case of Polytechnic, the admissions are based on the rank obtained in POLYCET.

Category ‘A’

Admissions under Category ‘A’ (70%) are made by the Telangana Council of Higher Education through a common entrance test TS EAMCET.

Category ‘B’

Admissions under Category ‘B’ (30%) are made by the college based on merit as per the guidelines of TSCHE.

Lateral Entry into 2nd Year B.Tech

In addition to the above, diploma holders are admitted directly into the 2nd Year of B.Tech to the extent of 10% of intake based on the rank obtained in the Engineering Common Entrance Test (ECET), under lateral entry scheme conducted by Govt. of Telangana.

Post-Graduate Programs

In case of M. Pharmacy, the admissions are based on the rank obtained in GPAT, PGECET.
