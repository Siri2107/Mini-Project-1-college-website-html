DESCRIPTION OF BRIG

Brilliant Grammar School Educational Society's Group of Institutions - Integrated Campus (Faculty of Engineering and Faculty of Pharmacy) has Engineering and Pharmacy (Affiliated to JNTUH) colleges with EAMCET Counselling Code BRIG. The Brilliant Group of Technical Institutions is sphere headed by the young and dynamic Chairman, Sri KASIREDDY NARAYAN REDDY, highly accomplished person and an eminent educationist par excellence, who did yeomen service to the field of education in the state. The college is under the dynamic leadership of the veteran Director, Dr. S PENIEL PAUL DOSS, M.Tech (CAD), Ph.D and Principal,Dr. CHAMAKURI KANTLAM , M.Pharm (Pharmaceutical Chemistry), Ph.D, who have more than 18 years of experience in Technical Education. Along with many experienced and qualified faculty, the academic activities are carried out effectively in all the Brilliant Group of Technical Institutions. The students academic performance is given top priority. monitored and counselled for best result.
Brilliant Group of Technical Instructions been contributing in the mission of transforming rural India into developed nation by running the Institutions with modernization, vision, human intelligence and persistence.
